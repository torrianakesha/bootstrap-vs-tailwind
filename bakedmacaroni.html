Baked Macaroni

Back to Home

Baked macaroni is a comforting pasta dish layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and perfectly cooked macaroni. It’s a favorite for gatherings because it’s hearty and easy to prepare.

This recipe creates a warm, cheesy, and satisfying meal. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or potlucks.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni pasta
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the macaroni until tender.
  2. Sauté onion and beef.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings.
  4. Mix with pasta and top with cheese.
  5. Bake until golden.
